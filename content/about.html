About

A person coming out from under a rock for the first time reviews ubiquetous ideas and objects they encounter.

What can happen if you decide to build your life only from the elements you select?
In these reivews, a 23 year old considers how they want to live in an age of too much information.
Under the rock is here to make the process of a selection more enjoyable and more lucid.
